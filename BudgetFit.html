When set, Budget becomes the client's all-inclusive number — e.g. type ₹5,80,000 with 10% install, and the engine works backward so equipment + installation lands exactly there. Leave blank for Budget to mean equipment only, as before.

The engine picks one model per category, upgrading and downgrading until the equipment total lands as close to the budget as possible. Surround and Atmos speaker quantities scale with the channel selection — 7.1 uses two pairs of surrounds, 9.1 uses three; Atmos configs add height speakers on top. In HTiB mode the box covers a 5.1 setup on its own; picking 7.1+ adds an extra surround pair, and picking an Atmos config adds in-ceiling speakers, on top of the box. Installation/service lines and GST are added on top of the equipment budget.
